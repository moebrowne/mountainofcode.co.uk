## How To Scrape Headers From 35K Sites

```
ppp smallweb.txt 'curl --max-time 5 --compressed --silent --location --head %'
grep --ignore-case --recursive --extended-regexp --no-filename 'x-powered-by:' results | sort | uniq
```

`ppp` is a tool of my own design. It enables running many tasks in parallel while keeping track of overall progress, you
can read more about it [here](/parallel-processing-with-progress).
