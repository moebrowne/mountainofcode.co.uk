# Iron Bloom - Pixel Art

#project
#devlog


Much has changed since the [first iteration](/iron-bloom) of Iron Bloom, but the most noticeable is the art. Art and
design are very much not my strong suit, I gave it a go so that I had more than block colours to look at while testing,
but it was poor to say the least.

![](/images/iron-bloom-pixel-art-0.png)

Unlike me [Iain](https://itmecho.com/) has some actual skill when it comes to pixel-art and created a proper sprite
sheet and made it look *awesome*. This was exactly the kind of look-and-feel I envisaged when first planning the
project.

It surprised me how motivating it was to have some proper sprites, it really started to feel like a proper game!

![](/images/iron-bloom-pixel-art-1.png)

Oh, and there are now chests too!
