# Heat Sink Fan Duct

#3D model

![](/3d/inverter-fan-duct--preview.png)

This model funnels the air flow of a 60 mm computer fan through a 40x90 mm heat sink. The whole thing is then mounted to
the wall. The design is optimised for simple printing without any support.
