Happy Pi day 2026!

I wanted to write a little simulation which (badly) approximates Pi. It's called the [Monte Carlo method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pi#Monte_Carlo_methods)
and simply involves placing a circle inside a square and then randomly placing points anywhere inside the square.

The ratio of points outside the circle to inside is ¼π. This isn't an animation, it's a [streamed image](/php-jpeg-stream)
of the simulation, refresh to get a new run. I love how randomness can lead to Pi.
