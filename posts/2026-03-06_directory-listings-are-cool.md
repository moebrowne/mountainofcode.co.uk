# Directory Listings Are Awesome 😎

#web
#minimalism


Directory listings have a bad reputation, they're ugly and are usually used to expose private data. What I love about
them is when they are used to distribute software or data. Want to find a specific version of NodeJS, easy: <https://nodejs.org/dist/>

![](/images/directory-listing-nodejs.png)

How about RaspiOS?

![](/images/directory-listing-rpi.png)

There's no huge app that takes ages to download, no ads, no JavaScript! It's fast, it's simple, it's elegant, it gets
out of your way. It always was and always will be.
