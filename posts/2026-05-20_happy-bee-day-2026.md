# Happy World Bee Day 🐝

#nature
#bees


> World Bee Day is celebrated on 20 May. On this day Anton Janša, the pioneer of beekeeping, was baptized in 1734.

<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Bee_Day>


To mark the occassion I've set up another solitary bee hotel, it contains both 6mm and 8mm tubes. The smaller tubes are
preferrered by mason bees.

![](/images/bee-hotel-2.jpg)

The tubes are sealed at one end with tissue, as [I learned last year](/bee-hotel-gaps) is necessary. I will fill the
rest of the space with more tubes over the coming days. They are housed in a large old can, this will keep the rain and
light out.


My existing one is filling up nicely, 4 'rooms' entirely filled already and plenty of the others are partly filled 😊

![](/images/bee-hotel-filling-up.jpg)

Also, I learned that the adult bees will rest in the hotel overnight and when the weather is cold or wet:
