# Angled Pipe Coupler

#3D model


![](/images/angled-coupler.jpg)

An angled male-female pipe coupler.

Beware of using PVC pipes on the female side, PVC expands when it gets hot and will put a lot of force on the coupler.

The pipe sizes, angles, etc. are configurable in the SCAD file.
