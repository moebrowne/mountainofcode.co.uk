# Monitor Arm Extension

#3D model

![](/images/monitor-arm-extension.jpg)

Short extension designed to adapt [this monitor stand](https://www.amazon.co.uk/gp/product/B01MR397OH) so that it holds
a pair of 21.5" monitors exactly next to one another.

The extension is 60 mm long, 20 mm wide and 33 mm tall, with boltholes 9 mm in diameter. All these attributes are
adjustable in the SCAD file if you need something custom.
